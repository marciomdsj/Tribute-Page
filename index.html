Carl Edward Sagan

Professor, Physicist, scientist, biologist, astronomer, astrophysicist, cosmologist, writer, scientifc popularizer and American activist

Carl Sagan was a unique person in the world of science. He had dreams like: Ending common problems in emerging countries with science and the scientific method.

He preserved the science mentality in witch the experimentation and the search for error are indispensable.

His most important awards:

  • Miller Research Fellows
  • Project Apollo
  • Nasa medals
  • Peabody Award
  • Emmy Award (Cosmos)
  • Masursky Award
  • Isaac Asimov Award

The cosmos series was first published in 1980, Cosmos brings together some of the most advanced knowledge of the time about nature, life, and the universe and stands to this day as one of the most important science popularization works in history.
